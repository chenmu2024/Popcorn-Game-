Game Description

Welcome to Popcorn Fun Factory! Step into the role of a factory manager in this engaging simulation popcorn game. You're in charge of your very own popcorn production facility, overseeing the entire process from raw kernel to delicious finished snack.

Take command of the production workflow, make crucial management decisions, and strive for top-quality output to keep your customers coming back for more. You'll need to skillfully balance production speed with careful precision as you operate diverse machinery and tools to craft the ultimate popcorn batches.

How to Play:

Pay close attention to the on-screen prompts to guide your operation of the popcorn equipment. Your mouse is your primary tool for interacting with all factory components. Successfully complete customer orders with accuracy and timeliness to accumulate points and unlock new levels of challenges.
