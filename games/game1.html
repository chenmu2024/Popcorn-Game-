Game Description

"Popcorn Collector" is a fun casual game where you control a character collecting popcorn in various entertaining scenes. The game features beautiful graphics and simple, intuitive controls suitable for players of all ages.

Collect popcorn, avoid obstacles, and complete level goals within the time limit. Each level offers different challenges and surprises waiting for you!

Game Features:

  • Beautiful game visuals and smooth gaming experience
  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Diverse levels and challenges
  • Fun sound effects and background music
  • Leaderboard system to compete with players worldwide

How to Play:

Use the keyboard arrow keys to move your character, space bar to jump, and collect popcorn throughout the scene. Avoid obstacles and complete objectives within the time limit.
