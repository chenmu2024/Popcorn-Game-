Popcorn Eater

Game Description

Skillfully guide the popcorn into the hungry hero's mouth. Collect coins and purchase upgrades. This charming casual game combines skill and timing with a delightful popcorn-themed experience.

How to Play:

Tap the bucket to release the popcorn. Time your release perfectly to land in the hungry character's mouth. Collect coins to purchase upgrades that will help you feed more popcorn and earn higher scores!
